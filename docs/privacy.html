Privacy Policy

ImageFlow

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Introduction

ImageFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Important: ImageFlow does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Data from third-party services

How We Use Your Information

All processing in ImageFlow is used solely for app functionality on your device:

  • Conversion: Converting images between supported formats
  • Resize: Applying local resize options you select
  • Local Save: Writing exported files to device storage you control

Your data is NEVER:

  • Sent to external servers
  • Shared with third parties
  • Used for advertising or marketing
  • Used for tracking or analytics
  • Accessible to us (the developers)

Local Storage and Security

  • Files are processed locally in the app sandbox
  • No cloud synchronization is required
  • You explicitly choose what to import, convert, and share
  • Files leave your device only when you manually share them

Permissions

Photo Picker Access (iOS/iPadOS)

  • Purpose: Select images from your photo library
  • Usage: Only when you explicitly choose photos
  • Scope: System picker limits access to selected items

File Access

  • Purpose: Import files and save converted outputs
  • Usage: Only when you choose files/folders in app flows

All permissions are optional and only used when you initiate the corresponding action.

Children's Privacy

ImageFlow does not knowingly collect information from children. The app is designed for general use and does not require personal accounts.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the Last Updated date and related app or documentation updates.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/Rodriqe/ImageFlow-Docs
  • Issues: https://github.com/Rodriqe/ImageFlow-Docs/issues

Summary (TL;DR)

  • All image processing happens on YOUR device
  • No data collection, no tracking, no ads
  • No servers, no cloud, no third parties
  • You have complete control over imported and exported files

ImageFlow - Your images stay on your device.
